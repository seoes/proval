title: "Welcome to Proval"
description: "What Proval is and who it is for."
date: 2026-06-01
---

Proval is a self-hosted bridge between your Git host and an LLM. Connect GitLab, Forgejo, or GitHub, point it at an OpenAI-compatible API, and let it review merge requests on your infrastructure.

This site and the product are still early. Expect rough edges, and please share feedback on GitHub if something does not work for your team.
